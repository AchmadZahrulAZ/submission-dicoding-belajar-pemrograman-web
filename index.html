Favourite Character

Basheeny

Basheeny is from Alchemist Code(Origin : Desert Zone) with dark element. His Unique Job is Mobile Knight and you can upgrade to Shadow Guardian. He has so many skill with Slash Type Attack and high mobility

Xiao

Xiao is from Genshin Impact. He is Yaksha Adeptus who defends Liyue with Anemo Vision. His weapon is Polearm. "Conqueror of Demons" and "Vigilant Yaksha" is the other Xiao's name

KaedeharaKazuha

Kaedehara Kazuha is from Genshin Impact. He is a Wandering Samurai from Inazuma who is currently with Liyue's Crux Fleet. His weapon is sword and have Anemo Vision.

Lunox

Lunox is from Mobile Legends Bang Bang. Lunox is a Mage. Her role is flexible right now, she can be a jungler, or maybe midlaner, goldlaner is also good. "The Enlighted One", "Twilight Goddess", and "The Mysterious Maiden" is the other Lunox's name

DarkNyx

Dark Nyx is from Alchemist Code(Origin : The Luxuria Belt Region) with wind element. His Unique Job is Chronometric Sorcerer of Sloth. Honestly, Dark Nyx is nice for DPS because he have Burst AOE Skill Magic Wind Attack and the other is buff, control, and debuff skill

FutureKnight

Future Knight is from Guardian Tales. Future Knight is the "Future" version of our main character in Guardian Tales. Her element is Basic, the role is Warrior, and can use many equipment like One-Handed Sword, Two-Handed Sword, Bow, Rifle, Shield, & Accessory

Sol

Sol is from Alchemist Code(Origin : Lost Blue) with dark element. He has so many potential in this game because Sol have skill with missile and magic damage. He can also heal & buff himself, debuff enemy, and teleport. His Unique Job is Truthsayer and you can upgrade to Truthmaster
